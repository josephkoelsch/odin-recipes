Yummy Pancakes

Description

I'm not much of a cook, but even I can make the most delicious and fluffy pancakes from scratch. All you need is a few ingredients you probably already have in your home, a skillet, a stove, and a little bit of patience. Check out the recipe below to learn all about it.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour 1 cup
  • Granulated sugar 1/4 cup
  • Baking Powder 2 tsp
  • Baking Soda 1/4 tsp
  • Salt 1/2 tsp
  • Butter (melted) 2 tbl
  • Egg (beaten)1 large
  • Milk 1 cup
  • Vanilla Extract 1 tsp

Directions

  1. Add all dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt) to large bowl. Mix thoroughly using whisk.
  2. Add wet ingredients to bowl (butter, egg, milk, vanilla extract). Mix with whisk until smooth.
  3. Set batter aside to rest for 2 minutes. While batter rests, place skillet on stove over medium low heat (you want the skillet to be hot when you start to avoid that "first pancake" effect).
  4. Grease pan with butter or cooking spray.
  5. Scoop about 1/4 cup of batter onto pan.
  6. When bubbles are popping and edges are crisp, it's time to flip.
  7. Cook until other side is golden brown.
  8. Remove from skillet and serve immediately with fresh maple syrup and berries.
