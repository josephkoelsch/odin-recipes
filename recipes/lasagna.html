Delicious Lasagna

Description

Wow your friends and family with the delicious lasagna that is incredibly easy to make.

Ingredients for Meat Sauce

  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil 2 tsp
  • Ground beef 1 pound
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced 3/4 cup
  • 1/2 large bell pepper (green, red or yellow), diced 3/4 cup
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Tomato Sauce 28 ounce
  • Tomato Paste 3 oz
  • Crushed Tomato 14 oz
  • Chopped fresh oregano 2 tbl
  • Chopped fresh parsley 1/4 cup
  • Italian seasoning 1 tbl
  • Garlic Powder 1 pinch
  • Red or white wine vinegar 1 tbl
  • Sugar 1/4 cup
  • Salt to taste

To assemble lasagna

  • Dry lasagna Noodles 9 unbroken
  • Ricotta cheese 15 oz
  • Mozzarella cheese grated or sliced 1.5 lbs (24 oz)
  • Grated Parmesan 1/4 lbs (4 oz)

Directions

  1. Put a large pot of salted water on stovetop on high heat. Prepare the sauce while water is heating.
  2. In a large skillet, heat 2 tsp olive oil on medium high heat. Add ground beef and cook until it is lightly browned on all siced. Drain all but 1 tbl of fat.
  3. Add diced bell pepper and onion to the same skillet. Cook for 4-5 minutes until the onions are translucent and the peppers soft. Add minced garlic and cook 30 seconds more. Return ground beef to pan. Stir to combine, reduce heat to low and cook another 5 minutes.
  4. Transfer been to a medium sized pot. Add crushed tomato, tomato sauce and tomato paste. Add parsley, oregano and italian seasoning, adjusting to taste. Sprinkle with garlic powder to taste. Sprinkle with red or white wine vinegar. Stir in sugar, 1 tbl at a time to taste. Add salt to taste, and note that you will later add parmesan, which is salty. Bring sauce to simmer and lower heat to maintain a low simmer. Cook for 15 to 45 minutes, stirring often. Remove from heat.
  5. Add dry lasagna noodles to the water you started heating in step 1. Cook noodles, stirring often, until al-dente per package directions. Drain in a colander and rinse with cool water, gently separating noodles. Spread olive oil in a large rimmed baking sheet, and lay out cooked Noodles on sheet turning them so that all sides are coated.
  6. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.

  7. In a 9 x 13 inch casserole or lasagna dish, ladle a cup of sauce and spread it over the bottom of the dish. Arrange one layer of lasagna noodles lengthwise (about 3 long noodles) over the sauce. Ladle a third of the remaining sauce over the noodles.

    Sprinkle a layer of a third of the mozzarella on top of the lasagna sauce. Add half of the ricotta cheese, bu placing dollops every couple of inches. Sprinkle half of the grated parmesan cheese evenly over the top of the ricotta cheese.

    Apply the second later of noodles and top it with half of the remaining sauce. Add half of the remaining ricotta cheese, and another half of the remaining parmesan.

    Finish with another layer of noodles. Spread the remaining sauce over the top layer of noodles and sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella cheese.

  8. Cover the lasagna pan with aluminum foil, tented slightly. Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes. Uncover for the last 10 minutes for a crusty top.
  9. Allow the lasagna to cool for at least 15 minutes and serve!
