Taco Tuesday!

Description

Who doesn't love a good taco? These are easy to make crowd pleasers great for Tuesday or really any day of the week!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef 1 lb
  • Chili powder 1 tbl
  • Salt 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin 3/4 tsp
  • Dried Oregano 1/2 tsp
  • Garlic Powder 1/4 tsp
  • Onion Powder 1/4 tsp
  • Tomato Sauce 1/2 cup
  • White corn tortillas 8
  • Canola or vegetable oil 1/2 cup

Directions

  1. Cook Ground Beef:Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add ground beef. Break up with a wooden spoon while cooking. Cook ground beef fully until no pink is visible.
  2. Drain:Drain excess grease from skillet. Return to stove and reduce heat to low.
  3. Season:Add 1/2 cup tomato sauce and taco seasoning. Stir together until meat is coated.
  4. Simmer:Simmer for 5 minutes
  5. Fry Tortillas: Pour 1/2 cup oil in a medium skillet. Heat over medium high heat. Carefully dip a tortilla, if the oil sizzles and bubbles, then it's hot enough. Gently lay the tortilla in the oil and fry each side for 30 seconds.
  6. Shape Tortillas: Place tortilla on paper towel. Carefully fold the tortilla to create a taco shape.
  7. Toppings: Fill the tortillas with ground beef and add desired toppings.
